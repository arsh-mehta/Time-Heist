How To Play Time Heist

The player will time travel and find hidden gems and dodge the parasites acid on each level. The player needs to find 4 stones. The time gem, leap gem, Weapon gem, cosmos gem.

Instructions

  • You Can Move With The arrow Keys.
  • You can press 'T' to Time Travel when you Time Gem.
  • You can Press Spacebar To jump when you get the leap gem.
  • You can use the weapon with the Right Click / Q Button when you have the weapon Gem.
